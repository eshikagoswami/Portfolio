Projects

  • American Sign Language Detection:Translating sign language gestures from live videos into alphabets using OpenCV.This project concludes live video frames are successfully captured from the webcam using OpenCV. The HandTrackingModule is effective in detecting hands within the video stream, allowing for the simultaneous detection of up to two hands (maxHands=2) and the ClassificationModule, utilizing the pre-trained CNN, accurately recognizes 26 ASL gestures corresponding to the English alphabet where hand photographs are taken and processed at the specified dimensions of 300x300 pixels (imgSize = 300). Bounding boxes (bbox) are successfully extracted from the original video frame for each detected hand.
